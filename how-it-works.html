How does this work?

Click the "Generate Strat" button, and a random strategy will be chosen from our database. Strategies are categorized into Tactical, Fun, and Challenge types.

How to Use:

1. Select Your Difficulty

Use the difficulty slider to choose how hard you want your strategy to be (from 1 - Easy to 5 - Hard).

2. Generate a Random Strat

Click the "Generate Strat" button to get a random strategy from the selected difficulty level.

3. View the Strategy Details

Click on the generated strategy to open the modal and view the strategy's detailed information, including tips and weapon recommendations.

Tactical Strategies

Strategies designed for serious competitive play, focusing on winning matches with coordination and precision.

  • Knives only! No guns.
  • No abilities, only gunplay.

Fun Strategies

Strategies focused on having fun with friends, doing something unconventional but still enjoyable.

  • Everyone buys a Sheriff and walks everywhere.
  • One player leads, others follow no questions asked.

Challenge Strategies

Strategies meant to challenge your team and push you to think creatively or perform under pressure.

  • Everyone rushes mid, no stopping.
